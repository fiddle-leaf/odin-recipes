Shrimp Scampi

My favorite Shrimp Scampi recipe so far!

I like to turn Shrimp Scampi into a pasta dish, because why not? A lot of pasta dishes can be super heavy, but, rest assured, this one shall not be. It is light and easy.

I used this recipe from Cafe Delites with a twist. I make it as easy as possible to prepare. Let's get started!

Ingredients:

  • shrimp
  • thin spaghetti pasta
  • butter
  • minced garlic, lots of it
  • red pepper flakes
  • lemon juice
  • parsley

Steps:

  1. Start by cooking your noodles. Bring six cups of water to a boil and cook your noodles al dente.
  2. In a big sauce pan, sautee your shrimp on medium-high heat with butter until almost cooked. Remove from sauce pan.
  3. Lower the heat to low and cook your garlic with a bit more butter. Add lemon juice and noodles into the pan.
  4. Lastly, incorporate shrimp, parsley, and desired seasonings to the sauce pan. Mix until thicken and serve!
